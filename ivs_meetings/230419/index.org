** The infamous visibilities and "uv" plane

- As an inverse problem:
  - $$V = \Psi^{\ast} B \Psi$$
  - $$\tilde{B} = \tilde{\Psi} V \tilde{\Psi}^{\ast}$$
- Eigenvalue decomposition:
  - $$\tilde{B} = \sum_{i} \lambda_{i} ||\Psi v_{i}||^{2}$$
